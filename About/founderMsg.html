Message from Founder

Mr. Dipen Patel

Overseas education is a dream for many Indian students and we at NINE International take this responsibility to fulfil it by providing quality admission and visa counselling, training for entrance exams and all supporting services at one same place. This world is for all and nobody should confine their boundaries limited to their home country. Think, move and explore are the three main characteristics of an individual, who are courageous enough to push their boundaries and make it big one day.

With a clear vision and objective to provide quality service and education for ambitious students, NINE International assures students and their parents in selecting the right country, academic institution and course. To meet the aspirations of the students and their parents, we at NINE International, follow a dynamic education system with modern teaching and learning methods. We thrive to make the future of every student’s golden life through quality service and genuine counselling. We believe, that our combined efforts will aid students to be competent enough to cope-up at national and international levels. Learning is a never-ending process of life and we aim to provide you with an opportunity to be a part of various rich cultures around the world.
