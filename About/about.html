ABOUT NINE INTERNATIONAL

NINE International® was founded in 2005, with the aim to provide effective and genuine counselling regarding abroad education to aspiring students, who are willing to learn in an excellent environment with the perks of having a multicultural experience and a great lifestyle. NINE International® is happy to offer its varied services keeping in view people’s needs and aspirations of different backgrounds. It has taken leaps for a number of students to fulfil their wish to study at destinations like the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore, Dubai and Europe etc. NINE International gives a one-stop solution to people interested to study, invest and do business.

Our dedicated team of professionals ensures, that every client is well served and is recognized for their responsiveness and flexibility. This team has the latest knowledge of immigration legislation and is ready to give information on expected changes. As a result, every client receives a single-window solution to every concern, whether it is professional or personal. Every client receives a customized solution from the counsellors to empower them with the right information and necessary skills before they leave their country.

What we do:
  • We dispel all myths of students related to the choice of university, career, etc. and provide them with vital information.
  • We maintain transparency throughout the process of admission.
  • We offer invaluable counselling, inevitable visa and fiscal guidance, all FREE of cost for successful enrolment.
  • We encourage efficacious faculty-student interaction.
  • We diagnose student’s difficulties personally and give them remedies without delay.
  • We offer enough mock tests to instill confidence in students.
What we don’t:
  • We do not make false promises.
  • We do not mislead students to meet selfish ends.
  • We do not mislead universities to get students admitted.
  • We do not exercise any sort of pressure, whatsoever, on any of the students to make them stick to our words.
  • We do not succumb to any sort of pressure from any quarters.
  • We do not turn away deserving students, who fall short of certain requirements.
