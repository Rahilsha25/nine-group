PTE Overview

Pearson Test of English is a computer-based proficiency language test, which evaluates Writing, Listening, Reading and speaking skills.

There are 2 (two) formats of the PTE:

  • PTE Academic Test
  • PTE General Test

PTE Test is conducted all year.
The test score is valid for 2 years

The three parts of the test

Part 1: Speaking & Writing.
Part 2: Reading.
Part 3: Listening.
